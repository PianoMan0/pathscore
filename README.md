# Pathscore

> **Turn Your Walk Into Music**

## What Pathscore Tracks
Pathscore tracks:
- How far you've walked
- Which direction you're walking
- Your speed
- And your steps

(None of this data is shared with anyone, including @PianoMan0!)

### Supported Browsers
- Chrome/Chromium 64+
- Firefox 55+
- Safari 11+
- Edge 79+

## If it's not working...

### GPS Not Working
- ✅ Check location permissions (Settings → Privacy → Location)
- ✅ Go outdoors with clear sky
- ✅ Wait 15+ seconds for initial GPS lock
- ✅ Try again after a few minutes

### Audio Not Playing
- ✅ Check audio permissions
- ✅ Unmute device
- ✅ Verify volume is audible
- ✅ Click Start button to activate audio context

### Steps Not Detected
- ✅ Device must have accelerometer (most modern phones)
- ✅ Walk with normal arm swing
- ✅ Ensure device is not stationary
